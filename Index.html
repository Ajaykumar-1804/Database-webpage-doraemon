Doraemon:
A blue robotic cat from the 22nd century, whose ears were cut off by a rat, so he is very afraid of rats. He loves to eat Dora Cakes.
Nobita:
A lazy boy who struggles with his studies but has a very kind heart; Doraemon has come specifically to help him.
Shizuka:
Nobita's best friend, who is very sensible and smart in her studies.
Gian:
The strongest and most hot-tempered boy in town who loves to sing (even though his voice is terrible).
Suneo:
A wealthy and cunning boy who always boasts about his expensive toys.
